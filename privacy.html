DATA PRIVACY


1. Data Collection

We only store your email and payment status. We DO NOT store your chats, voice recordings, or screen data.


2. Local Processing

JARVIS runs locally on your machine. All API requests to Gemini/Mistral go directly from your IP to their servers.


3. API Keys

Your API keys are stored in your local browser storage (localStorage) or local configuration files. They are never sent to our servers.
